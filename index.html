Lina Qiu is a MS student majoring in Computer Science at New York University. Prior to joining NYU, she has studied at South China University of Technology for two years and then continued her BEng degree at The University of Edinburgh. For more information about her education experience, please visit her Bio .

She has developed a vast variety of interests on Computer Science, such as security, database, algorithms, networking, and machine learning. On each subfield, she has done some exploration, and now decide to take computer security as her main research area.
